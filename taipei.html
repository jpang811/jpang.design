LOCAL MOVEMENTS

The local arts and farm-to-table movement is evolving and making its presence at flea markets, local restaurants, refurbished historical complexes and pop-up stores.

We’ve also had the pleasure of experiencing the farm-to-table movement first hand at the more remote Yang Ming Shan National Park where they serve mantou (Chinese buns) freshly made from wheat, sweet potato and taro, and lightly marinated veggie produces.

THE SWEET ESCAPES

Yang Ming Shan National Park is one of many places where tourists and locals go just when you need a breather from the cityscape.
